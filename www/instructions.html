What is Blackout Poetry?

Blackout Poetry is like recycled poetry--taking something in its original form and re-imagining it into something new. Traditionally, blackout poetry involves paper with text, a black marker, and a mind open to possibilities. To make poetry, you would cross out any unwanted words with your marker, leaving only a few words visible that would come together to make a wonderful poem.

What to do?

01. After you hit the 'Let's Begin' button, you'll be taken to a page that prompts you to paste text. You can either go to your browser and copy text or write your own.

02. Make sure to delete any line breaks. You'll want to avoid having paragraphs if you want the best result.

03. Use the awesome tool bars to customize your text, and then draw over it!

04. Save your poem by taking a screenshot! To do so on an iPad, press the Home button and your Power button, and then let go at the same time. After that, go to your photo library and it should be there.

05. Start over!
